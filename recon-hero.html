Original Design Challenge

"Design a dark-mode competitive intelligence dashboard for "RECON" inspired by: 1. Superset.sh (darkmodedesign.com) — terminal-aesthetic multi-agent bento UI, deep near-black bg, electric violet/indigo accent, parallel workflow cards showing live agent activity 2. Linear.app (darkmodedesign.com) — near-black #08090A, systematic product UI, indigo accents, clean dense information hierarchy 3. Midday.ai (land-book.com) — "run without manual work" AI-first financial philosophy applied to competitive intelligence 5 mobile screens: bento-grid intelligence overview dashboard, rival profiles with AI health scores, live signal feed with priority filters, AI-generated reports library, and data source integrations manager."

Product Brief

Overview

RECON is an AI-powered competitive intelligence platform that automatically tracks, analyzes, and surfaces actionable signals about competitors — so product teams can respond to market changes before they become threats. Inspired by Superset.sh's parallel agent architecture and Midday.ai's "run without manual work" philosophy, RECON replaces the 4-hour-per-week analyst grind with always-on AI watching.

Target Users

  • Product Managers — tracking competitor feature launches and pricing changes
  • GTM Teams — monitoring market positioning and messaging shifts
  • Founders — maintaining a real-time view of competitive landscape
  • Strategy Analysts — generating weekly intelligence briefs without manual scraping

Core Features

  • Bento Grid Dashboard — at-a-glance overview: rival count, signal volume, threat index, top movers, AI insight card, and active watches
  • Rival Profiles — scored profiles (0–100 threat score) with trend arrows, activity logs, and custom tag taxonomy
  • Live Signal Feed — priority-filtered stream of competitor events (pricing, product, team, funding) with AI-extracted context
  • AI-Generated Reports — one-click weekly briefs, pricing analyses, and competitor deep dives compiled by AI from raw signals
  • Source Manager — connect and configure data sources: web crawlers, LinkedIn, GitHub, G2, news APIs, social monitoring

Design Language

Deep navy-black (#070B12) primary background inspired by Superset.sh's terminal aesthetic. Electric indigo (#6366F1) as primary accent — the "intelligence color." Cyan (#22D3EE) exclusively for live/real-time indicators. Signal colors follow a clear hierarchy: cyan=live, green=positive, amber=warning, red=critical, violet=AI-generated. Bento grid card system creates visual rhythm and allows varied information density across the dashboard.

Screen Architecture

  1. Overview — Bento grid with metric cards, threat index, top movers, AI insight, watches strip, quick actions
  2. Rivals — Filter tabs + scored rival cards with avatars, trend badges, activity preview
  3. Feed — Live signal stream with priority filter chips, expandable cards with context
  4. Reports — Generate CTA + report library with progress visualization and stats
  5. Sources — Integration toggles with scan frequency, active count, and data point stats
