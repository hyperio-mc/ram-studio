Why FRAME

Every project. Every
brief. Every deadline.

Editorial Dashboard

Bold typographic metrics front and center — health score, project count, deadlines. Inspired by Locomotive's confident display type approach.

Asymmetric Project Grid

A full-width editorial hero card for your most active project, with a 2-column grid below — unconventional layout that gives work the visual hierarchy it deserves.

Visual Timeline

Living milestones with a dot-and-track visualization. Current milestone pulses with a subtle ring — clear at a glance where you are in each project.

Client Brief OS

Numbered editorial sections, red accent bars, typographic hierarchy. Briefs read like premium documents — because they should.

Live Studio Pulse

Horizontal workload bars per team member with avatar clusters. Real-time activity feed. See who's overloaded and who has capacity — instantly.

Warm Cream Palette

F6F4EF — the same warm off-white used by Stripe Sessions 2026. Not pure white. Not grey. Warm enough to feel premium, neutral enough to let work shine.

FRAME

Typography as
design decision.

Inspired by Locomotive.ca — where the headline reads "🔶 Locomotive® Digital-first Design Agency 🍺🔞" in HelveticaNowDisplay at full bleed. FRAME applies the same editorial confidence: DM Serif Display for headings, Inter Black for metrics, restrained color palette where every splash of indigo or red earns its place.

◼
Bold indigo (#1B1BE0)Active projects, primary CTA, live indicators — used sparingly but unmistakably.
◼
Editorial red (#E03030)Deadlines, review status, emphasis. The accent that says "pay attention."
◼
Warm cream (#F6F4EF)The base — same temperature as Stripe Sessions 2026. Familiar but not sterile.
