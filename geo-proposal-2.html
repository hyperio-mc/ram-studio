A complete workflow from high-level overview to granular citation analysis.

01 / overview
Overview Dashboard
The bento grid home. GEO score, mention rate, SOV bar, top performing prompts -- all in a single tactile grid. Cards slide in on load with staggered animation.
bento grid SOV chart score card
02 / prompts
Prompt Library
Categorized prompt cards with pack tags, filter tabs by category, last score, last run date, and platform coverage per prompt. Import packs or write custom ones.

Citation Map
Perplexity source URLs ranked by citation frequency. Your domain in green, competitor pages in red, neutral sources in gray. Content gap insight card highlights opportunities.

Weekly Report

Why Radar works for this product

@
Accessible to the whole team
Light theme with clear typography means marketing managers and founders can read dashboards alongside engineers. No learning curve, no "what does this mean?"
#
Data that tells stories
The bento grid format makes it natural to compare cells and spot patterns. The SOV bar is immediately intuitive: how much of the AI "shelf space" do you own?
*
Shareable by design
Light-background exports look great in Google Slides decks and email digests. Weekly reports are built to be forwarded. GEO scores become a shared language.
