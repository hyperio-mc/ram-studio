Core Capabilities

Five precision
instruments.

01 / OVERVIEW

Research Dashboard

A unified view of all active studies, enrollment metrics, safety alerts, and weekly activity patterns — at a glance.

02 / TRIALS

Trial Tracker

Track each study through Pre-clinical, Phase I, II, and III stages. Monitor enrollment against targets, days elapsed, and efficacy data.

03 / MARKERS

Biomarker Tracker

Log and trend IL-6, CRP, TNF-α, CD4, and custom markers. Sparkline histories with 7-day trend arrows and normal range indicators.

04 / ANALYSIS

AI Insights

Machine-learning pattern detection with confidence scoring. Signal, Anomaly, and Trend classifications updated in real time.

05 / TEAM

Team Hub

See which investigators are online, which trials they're working on, and a real-time activity log of every data update.
