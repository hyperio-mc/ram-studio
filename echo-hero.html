THE PLATFORM

Operations for teams and agents.

⬡ PULSE

Circular Health Grid

12 services rendered as concentric pulse rings — outer glow, mid ring, inner fill, centre dot — each color-coded by status. Health at a glance, not a table.

✦ AI TRIAGE

AI Confidence Scores

Every incident carries an AI-generated root cause probability. "91% — DB connection pool saturation." Not just an alert — an insight, with evidence attached.

⊶ BRIEF

Plain-Language Postmortems

AI Brief generates structured postmortems in plain English — what happened, root cause, resolution, and Space Mono-formatted AI suggestions for prevention.

DESIGN INSPIRATION

Informed by Evervault + Linear

Two sources drove this design. First, Evervault's Customers page — featured on Godly.website — with its near-pure-black background (rgb(1,3,20)) and razor-sharp developer-tool precision. Second, Linear's homepage on Dark Mode Design, where the language "product development for teams AND agents" reframes AI as a first-class participant in every workflow.

↗ EVERVAULT — GODLY.WEBSITE
Near-pure-black bg rgb(1,3,20). Precision data typography for security engineering. Translated into #06070F — a slight navy tint to push depth.
↗ LINEAR.APP — DARKMODEDESIGN.COM
rgb(8,9,10) ultra-dark. "Teams AND agents" framing. AI-native language pushed throughout every surface of this design.
