Core features

built for the long haul

⬤

Stillness Sessions

Configurable deep-work intervals — 45, 60, 90, or 120 minutes. The interface fades to pure timer so nothing competes for attention.

◎

Focus Score

A depth metric calculated from session continuity, distraction events, and completion rate. Improves as your practice deepens.

⌁

Motion Breaks

Structured transition periods between sessions — with intentional prompts, breathing cues, and space to set your next intention.

≡

Session Log

A sparse, beautiful record of your stillness practice. Weekly bar charts, session streaks, and total focused hours.

◯

Rhythm Settings

Tunable focus/break ratios. Lock distractions, configure ambient sounds, and set your personal cadence.

◈

Ambient Mode

During sessions the screen dims to a near-invisible pulse. The phone becomes an object of intention, not distraction.
