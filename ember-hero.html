Capabilities

Go beyond listening.
Understand everything.

◈
AI Taste Engine
Ember learns your listening DNA — topics, formats, depth — and surfaces episodes with a personalised match score before you even search.
◉
Episode Insights
Every episode is broken into key ideas, notable quotes, and one actionable takeaway. Save them to your personal ideas library.
▷
Smart Playback
Chapter-aware scrubbing, speed controls, and AI-highlighted moments. Jump directly to the most cited passage in any episode.
▦
Listening Analytics
Streaks, genre breakdown, top shows, hours per week — glowing amber bar charts inspired by Neon Postgres.
◻
Ideas Library
Captured insights are searchable and taggable. Build a personal knowledge graph from hundreds of episodes.
✦
Topic Threads
Ember connects related episodes across different shows. Follow a topic like "mental models" across 40+ episodes from 12 podcasts.
