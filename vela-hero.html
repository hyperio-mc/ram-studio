HOW IT WORKS

Designed around your biology

◎
Sprint Sessions
Choose your duration — 25 to 120 min — and VELA guides you into deep work. The large, minimal timer stays out of your way until you need it.
≋
Flow Map
See when your brain is at its sharpest across the week. VELA builds a rhythm profile from your sessions — not from self-reported data.
◈
Rhythm Score
A weekly score measures consistency, depth, and recovery. Not productivity in hours — but the quality of your creative rhythm.
THE PHILOSOPHY
"Your brain is not a server. It doesn't perform under constant load. It needs contrast — stillness and motion — to think clearly."
