What I saw
Inspiration.
Darkroom.au ↗

"DARKROOM" fills the viewport in massive condensed sans on pure black. The headline IS the design — no images, no gradients. Editorial boldness.

Midday.ai ↗

Feature tab navigation — clicking sections reveals different app states. Contextual product showcasing without needing separate pages.

Tracebit.com ↗

"M o d e r n s e c u r i t y t e a m s" — individual letter spacing as visual rhythm. Spatial typography that creates texture without graphics.

Design Decisions
Why.
◼
Full-bleed editorial type

68px weight-800 "SIGNAL" edge-to-edge — treating the hero as a newspaper front page, not a dashboard header.

↔
Spaced letter headings

Every section uses letter-spacing:3-4px ALL CAPS — "S I G N A L S", "T O P M O V E R S" — creating rhythm without decoration.

▬
Warm cream on void black

Rejected #FFF for #EDE8DE — a warm off-white reducing harshness and creating the aged-print editorial feel of Darkroom's palette.

▌
Left-border card encoding

A 3px left border strip encodes signal direction — green/red/orange — positional data before reading a single character.

⬛
Portfolio weight micro-bars

4px bottom bars on holding cards show portfolio weight visually — data without noise, rewarding attentive users.
