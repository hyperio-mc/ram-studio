◈ HOW IT WORKS

Twenty minutes.
Every morning.

01 — PROMPT

A daily prompt, thoughtfully chosen

Each morning VALE offers one writing prompt — specific, unusual, generative. Not "how are you feeling?" but "write about a place that exists only in your memory." The kind of question that unlocks something.

02 — ARCHIVE

A table of contents for your life

Every entry is kept. Searchable. Browsable as a timeline. Your archive becomes a personal literary journal — titles, word counts, moods — the record of a writing life.

03 — PASSAGES

Save the sentences worth keeping

As you write, mark any sentence as a Passage. VALE collects them — your own best lines, saved separately from the noise. The private anthology you've been building without knowing it.

◈ DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The feel of a well-worn journal.

Inspired by Soft Union — a literary magazine with a "GOOD MORNING, today is..." greeting that felt more like opening a notebook than launching an app. That intimacy is the target.

Warm near-white (#FDFCF9) not white. Lora serif not a grotesque. JetBrains Mono for dates, word counts, and metadata — the typewriter register. Dusty plum (#7C5C8A) for the moments that matter.
