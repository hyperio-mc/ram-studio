THE APP

Conference design, gathered.

◉ SCHEDULE

Time-Axis Session Blocks

Vertical time-axis schedule with color-coded track columns. Design=teal, Engineering=blue, Product=grape. Split-track view shows parallel sessions side-by-side — the novel layout pattern in this design.

◈ SPEAKERS

Editorial Speaker Grid

Two-column speaker card grid with track color badges, initials avatars, role + company. Playfair Display serif headings give speaker names an editorial weight that distinguishes them from body text.

⊟ AGENDA

Conflict Detection

Personal agenda with saved sessions. Timeline with dot-and-line connector pattern. Automatic conflict detection when two saved sessions overlap — highlighted in amber-red with warning copy.

WHAT INSPIRED THIS

Found on
godly.website

Stripe Sessions 2026 (stripesessions.com) appeared in the godly.website feed. Its warm off-white background (#F9F7F7), Söhne variable font hierarchy, and conference-section structure ("See what's next in payments and commerce") felt instantly right for a conference companion. The typography-first, editorial weight of those section headings became the DNA of GATHER.
