Features

Everything your team's
thinking needs

From quick thought capture to AI-powered pattern synthesis — WEFT turns individual notes into collective intelligence.

✍

Quick Capture

Frictionless thought entry that automatically suggests relevant threads, tags, and connections using semantic search.

⌘

Thread Studio

Organise entries into evolving threads. Track progress, depth, and momentum. See which ideas are getting deeper vs. stalling.

◈

AI Weekly Digest

Surface recurring themes, measure intellectual momentum, and get distillation suggestions — automatically, every week.

⊕

Connection Finder

Semantic overlap analysis finds hidden links between threads you didn't know were talking to each other.

◎

Archive & Search

Full-text and semantic search across your entire writing history. Filter by tag, thread, date, or theme cluster.

☀◑

Light + Dark Modes

A warm paper-white light theme and deep warm dark theme — designed for long writing sessions at any hour.
