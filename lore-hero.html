DESIGN PRINCIPLES
Void Immersion
Near-black #07060D with a blue tint evokes the dungeon atmosphere. Dark enough to feel like a cave, light enough to read comfortably.
Semantic RPG Colors
Sage = healing, Red = damage, Fire = spells, Gold = rewards. Zero reading required — your eye finds the threat instantly in initiative order.
Bento World Density
The World Index packs six regions + mystery count + NPCs into a single scroll. Bento grid variable widths signal importance without headers.

PRODUCT BRIEF / PRD

OVERVIEW

LORE is a dark-mode mobile companion app for tabletop RPG players and dungeon masters. It brings together the critical components of campaign management — world-building, character tracking, session recaps, and real-time combat — in a single beautifully designed interface. The aesthetic marries the gaming darkness of Marblex (lapa.ninja) with the data clarity of Midday.ai (darkmodedesign.com), resulting in a "command center for your campaign."

WHAT I FOUND

Marblex (lapa.ninja, March 2026): A next-generation gaming and interactive ecosystem showcasing rich dark-mode gaming UI with vibrant purple and gold on near-black. Proved that dense gaming data can be visually stunning.

Midday.ai (darkmodedesign.com): Dark finance OS for one-person companies, featuring ultra-clean tabular data on deep dark backgrounds. The transactional row-density pattern translated perfectly to initiative lists and skill lists.

TRIONN (darkmodedesign.com): Bold editorial dark mode with strong contrast between muted surfaces and vivid accent hits — inspired the "Glow" atmospheric technique used throughout LORE.

TARGET USERS

  • Dungeon Masters managing complex multi-campaign worlds with dozens of NPCs, locations, and threads
  • Players who want their character sheet always at hand with HP tracking and skill quick-reference
  • Casual groups who want AI-assisted session recaps without taking notes mid-game

CORE FEATURES

  • Campaign Hub — Active campaigns with session count, party roster, AI session summary teaser, and XP tracker
  • World Bento Grid — Responsive bento layout of world regions and lore nodes; NPC index; quick-add lore entry
  • Character Sheet — Full stat block with ability scores, HP ring, AC, speed, skill proficiencies, equipped items
  • AI Session Log — GPT-generated narrative session recap, session statistics, and scrollable event timeline
  • Combat Tracker — Initiative order with HP bars, status effect badges, action tracking, one-tap "End Turn"

DESIGN LANGUAGE

  • Deep Void (#07060D) — A near-black with a slight blue tint evoking the deep dungeon and mystical atmosphere; darker than classic charcoal for maximum immersion
  • Rich Amethyst (#7C3AED) — The primary accent; color of magic and mystery; used for active states, borders, and primary CTAs
  • Warm Gold (#D4AF37) — Rewards, XP, legendary items; connotes treasure and achievement without being garish
  • Parchment Foreground (#F0EBD8) — Slightly warm off-white; evokes aged maps and manuscript; reduces eye strain vs pure white
  • Semantic Color System — Sage (healing/HP), Danger Red (damage/death), Fire Orange (spells/active abilities) create instant visual parsing in combat
  • Multi-layer Glow Orbs — 4-layer radial ellipse technique creates atmospheric depth behind surfaces, like the ambient light of torches on stone walls
  • Bento Grid World View — Inspired by the bento grid trend on godly.website; variable-width region cards pack high information density while maintaining visual interest

SCREEN ARCHITECTURE

  • S1 — Campaign Hub: Active campaign hero card (with party avatars), campaign list with status pills, AI session teaser, XP progress bar
  • S2 — World Index: 6-region bento grid, mystery counter card, NPC roster with alignment and role, add-entry CTA
  • S3 — Character Sheet: Name + class header, HP/AC/Speed trio row, 6-stat ability score blocks, skill proficiency list, equipped items
  • S4 — Session Log: AI narrative recap card (gold-bordered), 4-stat session summary row, timestamp event timeline with type badges
  • S5 — Combat Tracker: Round + action tracker, 5-combatant initiative order with HP bars and status badges, End Turn CTA
