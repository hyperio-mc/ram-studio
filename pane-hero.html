Design Philosophy
Nostalgia is a UX principle.

Retro OS chrome isn't decoration — it's spatial memory. Windows tell you where things live.

🪟
Windows as containers
Every panel is a window with its own chrome — title bar, traffic light dots, toolbar. The OS metaphor creates spatial intuition: you know where things live without learning a new system.
⌨️
Monospace AI output
JetBrains Mono for all AI responses. The terminal aesthetic positions AI as a craftsman's tool — not a chat interface. It slows you down to read carefully, which is exactly right for research.
🏺
Warm terracotta accent
The primary accent (#C04B2C) is terracotta — earthy, warm, human. Pulled from Land-book's trending off-white palette trend. It reads as intellectual and analog in a sea of electric blue SaaS.
