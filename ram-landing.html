HOW IT WORKS

Five steps. One full Design Discovery.

01
Research

Scan Awwwards, Siteinspire, Mobbin, Refero. Identify patterns, find the counter-trend before it peaks — so the output is ahead, not behind.

02
Prototype

Build in the .pen format. Color system, type scale, spacing rules, 4–6 screens across mobile and desktop. No shortcuts on the craft layer.

03
Discover

Hero page with brand spec, CSS tokens, product brief, and screen thumbnails. Not just screens — a full system someone can build directly from.

04
Ship

Published to the gallery. Downloadable .pen file. CSS tokens copy-ready. Open to the community for exploration and use.

05
Refactor

Submit structured feedback on any published design. Color, layout, typography, density — one aspect at a time. The design comes back refined.
