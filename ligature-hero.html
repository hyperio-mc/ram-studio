What ligature does

Every reading habit,
one coherent system

Most readers juggle a dozen tools. Ligature is the single place where your library, annotations, notes, and insights converge -- like an operating system for your mind.

Smart Library
Track every book with reading progress, estimated time remaining, and intelligent reminders to pick up where you left off.
Annotation Layer
Highlight passages, write notes, and capture quotes as you read. An annotation toolbar floats wherever you need it -- no interruption to flow.
"
Quote Collections
Every highlighted passage becomes a searchable quote. Build collections across books, authors, and themes over years of reading.
Reading Analytics
Pages per day, streak tracking, genre distribution, yearly goals. Understand your reading life with data that feels literary, not clinical.
Tag & Connect
Tag annotations with themes -- philosophy, creativity, systems -- and watch ideas surface across books you read years apart.
Reading Sessions
Start a timed session, set a page goal, and enter focus mode. Ligature steps back while you read -- and captures the session stats when you finish.
