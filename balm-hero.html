What BALM does

Five lenses on
one living body

Most trackers give you numbers. BALM gives you meaning — cross-referencing sleep, movement, nutrition, mood, and trends to surface what actually matters.

🌅
Morning Brief
A readiness score and key biometrics surfaced before you've touched a screen. HRV, sleep quality, body temp, and resting HR — all in one glance.
⚡
Movement
Activity rings, heart rate zones, and a workout log that respects how your body actually moved — not just steps and calories.
🌿
Nourishment
Intuitive food logging with macro tracking and hydration segmentation. Gentle nudges rather than rigid targets.
🫀
Reflection
Evening mood check-ins, a body scan, journal prompts, and gratitude practice — all connected to your biometric state.
📈
Trends & Correlations
Sparkline grids for 7-day patterns, and BALM's AI-surfaced correlations — what's actually causing what in your data.
✦
Editorial Warmth
Designed in a warm parchment palette with Libre Baskerville serif type — because your wellness data shouldn't feel clinical.
