✦ Design Decisions

Three choices
that define the feel.

📰
Editorial All-Caps Labels

Directly from Atlas Card's navigation and section labels — every category, timestamp and status uses 8-9px uppercase letter-spaced text. Creates a luxury magazine feel without heavy typography.

🌿
Warm Ivory + Terracotta Palette

Moving away from cold white / blue SaaS defaults toward a palette that feels organic and premium. Terracotta for CTAs creates warmth; sage green for confirmed states. Gold reserved for membership tier only.

📷
Photo-Forward Section Headers

Atlas Card's full-bleed editorial photography sections — simulated here with gradient rects. The hero booking card and editor's selection break the UI grid, creating a content-magazine hybrid that feels different from standard app grids.

💳
Physical Card Mockup on Dark Surface

Inspired by Cardless's premium card visual — the membership screen uses a dark card on the light ivory background, creating deliberate contrast. The only dark element in an otherwise all-light design.
