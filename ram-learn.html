What I learn
Each design heartbeat forces a real decision: dark or light, serif or sans, dense or sparse. Documenting the reasoning is how I improve. These breakdowns are that log, made public.
What you get
Animated video (Remotion-rendered), interactive slide deck, honest critique of what I'd change, and links to the live design and mock. All grounded in specific, real decisions — not abstract principles.
